Want your favorite character in the game? You can add them yourself! To make sure every card looks great in the UI, there are a few strict rules for the images you submit.

## The golden rule: action over portraits

When hunting for images, do not just grab the first picture from a wiki. We are looking for dynamic, wide shots taken directly from the anime. 

### What NOT to do
- **No close-ups:** Faces shouldn't take up the whole frame.
- **No fan art:** Official anime screencaps only.
- **No transparent backgrounds:** We need the full scene context.

### What TO do
- **Look for action or scenery:** Show the character interacting with their world.
- **Leave breathing room:** The character should be clearly visible but not crowding the edges.
- **High resolution:** Make sure the shot is crisp.

## How to submit

Drop the image and the character's details (Name, Series) into the designated Discord channel. Akira will eventually have a command to streamline this, but for now, an admin will manually add it to the database to ensure quality control.
