title: What is Akira?
description: A digital card collection system on Discord - collect, trade, and chat about anime with your community.
---

**Akira** is a digital card collection system you use through **Discord**. Your server drops, grabs, trades, and burns cards in chat, so people can connect over the anime and characters they care about-not just idle in voice or off-topic channels.

When you are ready to play, [Your first card](/getting-started/your-first-card) walks through a typical [drop](/commands/drop) and [grab](/commands/grab). [Importing from Karuta](/getting-started/importing-from-karuta) covers spreadsheets. Economy basics live under [Bits](/economy/bits), [Server buff pool](/economy/server-pool), and [Personal milestones](/economy/personal-milestone).
