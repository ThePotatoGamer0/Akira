---
title: Your first card
description: React on a drop, say hi to Akira, and welcome the card into your binder.
---

When a friend runs [drop](/commands/drop), three cards appear — react with 1️⃣ 2️⃣ or 3️⃣, then use [grab](/commands/grab) to claim the matching slot while the [drop](/commands/drop) is still live.

## After the grab

- Browse what you own with [collection](/commands/collection) (the WebUI grid is great for big binders).
- Try [daily](/commands/daily) for starter bits and check [cooldowns](/commands/cooldowns) before you chain grabs.
- If you are hunting someone specific later, [wishlist](/commands/wishlist) and [lookup](/commands/lookup) pair nicely.
